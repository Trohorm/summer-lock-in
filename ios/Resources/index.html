Running around the lifting plan

Best slots: Sunday and Wednesday (rest days). Keep most running easy — you should be able to talk in full sentences. Easy cardio helps recovery; hard cardio steals from it.

  • Easy / zone-2 runs 30–60 min are the default.
  • Keep Friday easy so Saturday legs are quality, and go easy the day before Lower Body too.
  • Intervals or hard runs: max 1× per week, ideally Sunday, not the day before a leg day.
  • If legs feel dead in the gym, cut running volume before cutting lifting.
